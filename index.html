Tired of perusing Craigslist and having to spend hours sifting through hoards of scammy ads posted by real estate brokers you just know you don't want to have to deal with in person?

Tired of seeing the same ads over and over again because someone felt like they had to post it 50 times that day?

Tired of going to see the same apartment twice because two different brokers took completely different pictures of the place, none of which look like the actual place?

It's time for a change.

With Apartment Hunt NYC you'll be able to:

  • Systematically keep track of the apartments you've seen or want to see
  • Rate brokers and the apartments they show
  • See apartments on a map instead of through disorganized classified ads
  • And much more! YOU dictate the features you want

As a reward for your backing of this project, you'll receive a 2-year premium membership (that means more useful features) to the site for free. Hopefully the site works really great for you and you won't have to move too often.

FAQ

Who will post listings on the site?
The listings will be created by you! You'll be able to make a listing either public or private (if you like an apartment so much you don't want to share), and you'll be able to see others' public listings. The idea is that you'll provide the most accurate information about each place, and everyone will save time because they won't have to go and see apartments they hope might be nice.
Can the site be used for other cities?
The site will initially be for New York City, because apartment hunting here is a whole nother beast. It will easily extend to support other cities in the future.
